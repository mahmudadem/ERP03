# AI Assistant Chat Sidebar

**Date:** 2026-05-13
**Status:** Implemented

---

## What It Does

The AI Assistant chat sidebar shows a list of your recent conversations. Each item displays:

- **Conversation title** — auto-generated from your first message (up to 50 characters)
- **Message count** — how many messages are in the conversation
- **Last active timestamp** — when the conversation was last used

If a conversation has no title yet (rare), it falls back to showing "New conversation".

---

## How to Use

1. Click the **☰ menu icon** in the top-left of the AI chat widget (or the sidebar toggle on the full page)
2. The sidebar shows your recent conversations grouped by time (Today, Yesterday, Older)
3. Click any conversation to load it
4. Click **New Chat** to start a fresh conversation
5. Click the **trash icon** to delete a conversation

## Technical Notes

- Conversations are loaded from `GET /tenant/ai-assistant/conversations`
- Titles are auto-generated by the backend (`SendChatMessageUseCase.generateTitle()`)
- The sidebar uses `ConversationMetaDTO` with `title`, `messageCount`, `lastMessageAt`, `createdAt`
- Both the floating widget (`GlobalAiWidget`) and the full page (`AiAssistantHomePage`) have sidebars
