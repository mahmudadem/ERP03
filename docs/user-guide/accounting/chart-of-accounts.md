# Chart of Accounts

The Chart of Accounts (COA) is the backbone of your accounting system. It provides a structured list of all the financial accounts used by your organization to record transactions.

## Navigating the COA

When you open the Chart of Accounts, you will see a structured list of your accounts organized in a tree hierarchy. 
- **Header Accounts**: These act as folders to group related accounts (e.g., "Assets").
- **Posting Accounts**: These are the actual accounts where financial transactions (journal entries) are recorded (e.g., "Cash - Head Office").

## Quick Filtering

To quickly find specific accounts, use the quick filter bar at the top of the list. Click on any of the category pills to filter the view:
- **All Classes**
- **Asset**
- **Liability**
- **Equity**
- **Revenue**
- **Expense**

You can also use the search bar to find accounts by code or name.

## Viewing Account Details (Drilldown)

To view detailed information about an account, simply **click on its row** in the list. This will slide open the **Account Ledger Drilldown** panel on the right side of the screen.

The drilldown panel displays:
- **Account Summary**: Includes the account type, classification, current operational value, status, and any descriptive notes.
- **Journal Posting Entries Audited**: A detailed list of recent journal entries recorded against this account, showing debits, credits, and the calculated ledger balance.

## Managing Accounts

Hover over any account row to reveal quick action buttons:
- **Add Sub-Account (+)**: Quickly create a new child account under the selected header account. Note: You cannot add child accounts under a posting account.
- **Edit**: Modify the details of the selected account.
- **Deactivate (Trash)**: Deactivate the account if it's no longer in use. Protected or active accounts might require additional steps to deactivate.

Use the **Expand All** and **Collapse All** buttons at the top to easily navigate deep account hierarchies.

## Expanding and Collapsing the Tree

Select the round arrow beside a header account to show or hide its child
accounts. The control has a larger click area for easier selection.

- In Arabic, a collapsed account points left toward its hidden child branch.
- In English and Turkish, a collapsed account points right.
- An expanded account points down.
- **Expand All**, **Collapse All**, and **New Account** remain on one line for
  clearer operation.
