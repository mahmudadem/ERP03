# Purchase Vendor Groups

Vendor Groups let you classify suppliers into simple segments such as local suppliers, import suppliers, service providers, or subcontractors.

## What vendor groups do

- Help organize vendors for filtering and reporting.
- Make the vendor master easier to maintain when you have many suppliers.
- Let users assign one group to each vendor from the vendor card.

## What vendor groups do not do

- They do not change purchase invoice posting.
- They do not change Accounts Payable balances.
- They do not change tax, inventory valuation, payment status, or vouchers.
- They do not control price lists or approvals yet.

## Create a vendor group

1. Go to `Purchases -> Vendor Groups`.
2. Click `New Group`.
3. Enter a group name, such as `Local Suppliers`.
4. Add an optional description.
5. Keep status as `Active`.
6. Click `Save`.

## Assign a vendor to a group

1. Go to `Purchases -> Vendors`.
2. Open a vendor.
3. Open the `Commercial Terms` tab.
4. Select a `Vendor Group`.
5. Save the vendor.

## Delete a vendor group

You can delete a group only when no vendors still reference it. If the system blocks deletion, open the vendors assigned to that group, clear or change their group, then delete the group again.
