# Item UOM Selection on Documents

When you choose an item on a sales or purchase document line, the UOM picker shows only UOMs defined for that item.

It includes:

- the item's base UOM
- the item's sales or purchase UOM
- any active UOM conversions defined on the item card

Example: if an item has a BOX to PCS conversion, both BOX and PCS can be selected on sales and purchase lines.

To change which UOMs appear, open the item card and maintain the item's UOM conversions there. The document line
picker does not create new UOMs or conversions.
